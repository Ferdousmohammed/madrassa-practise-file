Welcome to Kaziarchar Hamidia Shukuria Alim Madrasha

On the first day of January 1938, the Kajiarchor Hamidia Shukuria Senior Madrassa opened its doors to students, the founding members were Md. Abdul Sukkur Madbar & Md. Hamizuddin Chokdar with Sir P.J. Hartog as the first Vice-Chancellor of the Madrassa. The Madrassa was set up in a picturesque part of the village known as Bilaspur on 5 acres of land [The establishment was shifted here after the madrasa collapsed in the river]. The Madrassa started its activities with 1 Faculty, 2 Departments, 3 teachers, 37 students and 1 dormitory (Halls of Residence) for the students. At present the Madrassa consists of 13 Faculties, 83 Departments, 12 Institutes, 20 residential halls, 1 hostels. The number of students and teachers has risen to about 450 and 2022 respectively. The main purpose of the Madrassa was to create new areas of Elm knowledge and disseminate this knowledge to the society through its students. Since its inception the Madrassa has a distinct character of having distinguished scholars as faculties who have enriched the national pool of knowledge by making notable contributions in the fields of teaching. The Hamidia Madrassa of Elm is one of the oldest Madrassa in Greater Faridpur. The Institute conducts regular schooling programs like Alim, Fajil, and Under Graduation under the affiliation of the National Madrasa Board (Govt. Madrassa, Accredited 'A' Grade by MGC).
